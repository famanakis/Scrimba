Top things to do in Albuquerque

International Balloon Fiesta

This 9 day event has over 500 hot air balloons participating each year, and is the largest event of its kind in the world.

Sandia Peak Tramway

Stretching from Northeast Albuquerque to Sandia Peak, the Sandia Peak Tramway is the third longest in the world.

Albuquerque BioPark

Nestled by the Rio Grande River, the ABQ BioPark consists of: The ABQ BioPark Zoo, Botanic Garden, Aquarium and Tingley Beach.

Sandia Mountains

Full of recreational activities for all seasons: skiing, sledding, horseback riding, hiking, mountain biking, picnic areas.
